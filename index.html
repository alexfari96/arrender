AR Render - Privacy Disclaimer

Our app does not store any sensitive user data, below we will explain why we need permissions to access the Internet, the Camera and the Memory.
Access to INTERNET

We need Internet access to allow import from your Dropbox account and to show you the main online converter for your 3D models.

Access to CAMERA

We need to access the Camera to implement augmented reality and to allow you to take a picture of the scene. When you are not using the app it cannot access the camera.

Access to STORAGE

We need to access memory to locally store the files (models) you import into the app and the photos you take. The app does not access any files other than an imported model on the device.
